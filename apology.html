I'm So Sorry

I don’t even know where to begin — maybe with how much I miss you.
You were such a huge part of my journey, from our estate days to Cairo, and I hate that I let distance and silence build a wall between us.

Dropping out and coming home felt like everything was falling apart, and somewhere in the middle of my mess... I disappeared. But you didn’t deserve to be left out in the cold.

I know you're mad. You have every right to be. But I just want you to know: I still care deeply. I still remember your laughter, your kindness, and how much it meant to have you in my corner.

I hope this small note reaches you and softens the anger — even just a little. I hope it reminds you that you were never forgotten.

I’m sorry. I miss you. And I’d really love to reconnect.

— With love, always
